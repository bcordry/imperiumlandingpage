What is Emerald Imperium?

Emerald Imperium is a highly modified ROM hack of Pokémon Emerald, focusing on strategic battles, trainer difficulty scaling, team variety, and Nuzlocke challenge support. Every route has been rebalanced, every trainer reimagined, and the Pokédex has been revamped from top to bottom.

This hack aims to provide a fresh and challenging experience for veteran trainers while maintaining the charm and accessibility of the original game. Whether you're looking for a strategic challenge or a new way to experience Hoenn, Emerald Imperium offers something for everyone.

Complete control and customization for your Pokemon!
More in-battle information to help you play your best!
Battle additional traveling Gym leaders and rivals from outside of Hoenn!
Use the Dexnav and other overworld tools to find your perfect team!
Face stronger enemies with new Mega forms and more!
Overworld and Following sprites for all Pokemon!
Use your own strong megas against all sorts of regional forms!

Key Features

  • Enhanced Battles: Completely rebalanced trainer battles with improved AI and strategic team compositions
  • Expanded Pokédex: New Pokémon, forms, and evolution methods
  • Quality of Life: Modern conveniences like running indoors, quick save, and more
  • Difficulty Options: Multiple difficulty settings and Nuzlocke mode support
  • New Content: Additional events, sidequests, and postgame content
  • Battle Mechanics: Updated moves, abilities, and held items
  • Progressive Challenge: Adaptive difficulty that grows with your progress
  • Custom Music: New arrangements and battle themes
